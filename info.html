tips + faq


here are some tips to help you make stronger passwords and avoid common mistakes:

tips for strong passwords


  • use at least 12 characters whenever possible

  • mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols

  • avoid common words, phrases, or patterns

  • use a unique password for every account

  • consider using a password manager to generate and store passwords




frequently asked questions

  • q: how do i know if my password is strong?
    a: use the password strength tester on this site for real-time feedback.

  • q: can i remember all my passwords?
    a: consider using a password manager to safely store them.

  • q: is it safe to use generated passwords?
    a: yes, as long as you store them securely and avoid reusing them.
